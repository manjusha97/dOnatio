What You Can Donate

All of us have a lot of old things lying around at home which we cannot throw or cannot preserve. Even if you want to donate them, you wouldn't know if the seekers are fraudulent. dOnatio works as the bridge between the donor and the seeker & ensures that the seekers are authorized.

We collect donations from you and deliver them to an authorized and validated organization that will ensure to provide them to the seeker.
